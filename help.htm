Election Results

Online Help

The Election Results application provides a map-based view of voting results tabulated on election night for each polling division.

Using the Application

To display the results, enter an address or polling division number in the search box or click on the map. The polling division will be highlighted and the voting results will be displayed in the tab along the bottom of the map.

Address or Division Search

  1. Choose between address and division search mode
  2. Enter an address or division
  3. Press enter to search immediately or select a result from the autocomplete list.
Those using a mobile phone or older browser will need to select the Search icon
to display the search field.

My Location Search

Selecting the Geolocation icon
will conduct a search based on your current location.

Election Results

Once a division has been selected, the election results panel at the bottom of the page will display the following:

  • A graph of the results for each political contest as well as the current winning candidate or party
  • The voter turnout for the division

To view other political contests on the map, choose the contest to display at the bottom of the results panel.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share icon
to share your map in an email, a Facebook post or on Twitter.

Browser Compatibility

The Election Results application is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft Internet Explorer 7/8/9/10/11, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Apple Safari, iOS Safari 4.0+, and Android Chrome 2.3+.
